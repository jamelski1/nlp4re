The Problem

Manual analysis of natural language requirements is error-prone, time-consuming, and fails to scale.

The Solution

NLP4RE — applying Natural Language Processing techniques to automate requirements engineering tasks.

The Goal

Assist humans in processing requirements. Reduce ambiguity and improve quality.

Verdict: A Field in Rapid but Isolated Growth

Trend

Explosive Growth

Research output has surged dramatically since the early 2000s, with no signs of slowing.

Output

Solution Proposals > Validation

The community produces far more proposals than empirically validated results.

Technology

Syntax > Semantics

Most tools rely on basic syntactic NLP rather than advanced semantic understanding.

Community

Isolated / No Shared Data

Researchers work in silos with limited collaboration and no shared benchmarks or datasets.
